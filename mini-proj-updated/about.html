About Esports

Esports (electronic sports) refers to professional competitive video gaming. What started as small arcade competitions has grown into a global industry with millions of fans, sponsorships, and professional leagues.

Professional players train daily, mastering mechanics, strategies, and teamwork. Esports organizations operate like traditional sports teams, with coaches, analysts, and international tournaments filling entire arenas.

Popular Esports Genres

  • MOBA – Dota 2, League of Legends
  • FPS – CS:GO, Valorant
  • Mobile Esports – Mobile Legends, Wild Rift
  • Battle Royale – PUBG, Fortnite

Today, esports is one of the fastest-growing industries, blending gaming, entertainment, streaming, and technology — with events watched by millions globally.
