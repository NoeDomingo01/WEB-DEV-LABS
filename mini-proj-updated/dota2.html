What is Dota 2?

Dota 2 is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) developed by Valve. It is one of the most iconic esports titles in the world, known for its complex gameplay, strategic depth, and legendary competitive scene.

Two teams of five players compete to destroy the enemy's Ancient while defending their own. Teamwork, strategy, map control, and hero mastery are the keys to victory.

Why Dota 2 is iconic

  • Deep and complex gameplay
  • Over 120 unique heroes
  • Massive global championships (The International)
  • Record-breaking prize pools in esports history
  • Dedicated fanbase worldwide
