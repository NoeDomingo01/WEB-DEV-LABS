Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

The most popular MOBA in Southeast Asia and a global esports force.

MLBB in Esports

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB) is the biggest mobile MOBA esports title in Southeast Asia. With tournaments like the M-Series World Championship and Mobile Legends Professional League (MPL), MLBB has built a massive competitive scene.

Countries like the Philippines and Indonesia dominate the MLBB scene, with teams such as Blacklist International, ONIC Esports, ECHO, RRQ, and Bren Esports becoming international champions.

Why MLBB is Huge

  • Mobile-first esports title with global reach
  • Massive fanbase especially in Asia
  • Exciting fast-paced 5v5 gameplay
  • Strong professional league structure (MPL)
