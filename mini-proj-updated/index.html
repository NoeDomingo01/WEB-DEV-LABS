What is Esports?

Esports, short for electronic sports, refers to competitive video gaming played at a professional level. Millions of fans worldwide tune in to watch players compete in games like Dota 2, League of Legends, Valorant, CS:GO, and Mobile Legends.

These events are streamed live, hosted in massive arenas, backed by sponsorships, and feature prize pools reaching millions of dollars.
