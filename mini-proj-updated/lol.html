What is League of Legends?

League of Legends (LoL) is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) developed by Riot Games. It is one of the most played and watched esports games in the world, known for its fast-paced battles, strategic gameplay, and global competitive scene.

Two teams of five champions battle to destroy the enemy Nexus. Players select from over 160 champions, each with unique abilities, roles, and playstyles.
