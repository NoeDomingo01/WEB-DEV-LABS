League of Legends

League of Legends (LoL) is a MOBA developed and published by Riot Games in 2009. Known for its accessibility and depth, LoL quickly grew into one of the most played games in the world and remains a dominant force in esports.

Gameplay

In League of Legends, two teams of five players compete to destroy the enemy Nexus. Each player chooses a “champion” with unique abilities, ranging from tanks and assassins to mages and marksmen. The game emphasizes teamwork, strategy, and quick decision-making.

World Championship

The League of Legends World Championship, often called “Worlds,” is one of the most watched esports events globally. Featuring the best teams from every region, Worlds has filled arenas and even stadiums, with millions of fans watching live streams worldwide. Iconic moments, like Faker’s legendary plays, are part of esports history.

Esports Legacy

Riot Games has built a structured competitive scene with leagues like the LCK (Korea), LCS (North America), and LEC (Europe). These regional competitions lead up to international events, making League of Legends one of the most professionally organized esports ecosystems ever created.

With its global reach, exciting tournaments, and passionate fan base, League of Legends continues to define what it means to be a modern esport.
