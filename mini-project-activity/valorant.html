Valorant

Valorant, released by Riot Games in 2020, is a tactical first-person shooter (FPS) that blends precise gunplay with unique character abilities. Often compared to Counter-Strike: Global Offensive for its round-based format, Valorant introduces Agents—characters with distinct skills that can turn the tide of battle.

Since its launch, Valorant has quickly become a major title in the esports world. Riot Games built upon its success in League of Legends by creating the Valorant Champions Tour (VCT), a global competitive structure that culminates in the Valorant Champions event. With millions of viewers and massive sponsorships, Valorant has established itself as one of the premier esports.

The game’s mix of strategy, mechanical skill, and creative agent abilities makes it a fan favorite, both for casual play and high-stakes tournaments. Teams from North America, Europe, and Asia battle for dominance, with storylines and rivalries that keep fans hooked.
