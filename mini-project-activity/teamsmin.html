Top Dota 2 Teams

Dota 2 has been home to some of the most iconic esports teams in history. These organizations have left their mark by winning championships, developing legendary players, and inspiring millions of fans worldwide. Competitive Dota 2 is not just about gameplay—it is also about teamwork, discipline, and adapting strategies on the fly.

One of the most successful teams is OG, who made history by winning The International back-to-back in 2018 and 2019. Their incredible underdog story and team chemistry inspired countless players and fans. Another powerhouse is Team Secret, known for their consistent dominance in regional leagues and global tournaments. Chinese teams like PSG.LGD and Invictus Gaming also made huge contributions to Dota 2’s competitive history, proving that the game’s popularity spans across continents.
